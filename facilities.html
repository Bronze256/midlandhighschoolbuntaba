World-Class Facilities

Discover the exceptional learning environment at Midland High School Buntaba

Academic Facilities

Our state-of-the-art academic facilities provide the perfect environment for effective teaching and learning.

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-lit classrooms equipped with wide black boards and ergonomic furniture to enhance the learning experience.

Resource Center

Our extensive library houses over 1,000 volumes, digital resources, quiet study areas, and computer stations for research.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with modern equipment for practical experiments and research.

Computer Labs

A modern computer lab with high-speed internet, latest software, and 1:1 student-computer ratio for ICT education.

Auditorium

500-seat multi-purpose auditorium with professional sound and lighting systems for assemblies, performances, and lectures.

Creative Arts Studio

Dedicated spaces for visual arts, music, and drama with professional equipment and materials for creative expression.

Sports & Recreation

We believe in holistic development through physical education and competitive sports.

Football Pitch

Full-size football field for our students with a fully equipped school team.

Basketball Court

An outdoor court with floodlights for evening practice and competitions.

Athletics Track

Wide space for sprinting, hurdles, and long-distance running.

Indoor Games

Chess, and board games in our recreation center.

Technology & Innovation

Preparing students for the digital future with cutting-edge technology resources.

DIT Lab

Dedicated space for robotics, coding, and engineering projects with prototyping tools.

Wi-Fi Campus

High-speed wireless internet coverage across the entire school for research and learning.

Digital Library

Access to thousands of e-books, academic journals, and online learning platforms like shareability.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive boards in all classrooms for enhanced learning.

Boarding Facilities

Safe, comfortable, and conducive residential facilities for our boarding students.

Dormitories

Spacious dormitories with beds, personal storage, and common areas. Separate for boys and girls.

Dining Hall

Modern dining facility serving balanced, nutritious meals prepared by professional catering staff.

Health Center

Fully equipped medical facility with a full-time nurse and visiting doctor for student healthcare needs.

Counseling Center

Professional counseling services for academic, career, and personal development guidance.
