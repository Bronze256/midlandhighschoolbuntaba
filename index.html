About Our School

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Founded in 2018, Midland High School Mukono has established itself as one of Uganda's premier secondary institutions. Our mission is to provide quality education that develops well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of higher education and professional life.

With a focus on academic rigor, character development, and extracurricular engagement, we create an environment where students can thrive intellectually, socially, and morally.
